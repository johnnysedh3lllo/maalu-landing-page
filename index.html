What would you like to know about Maalu?

What is Maalu?

Maalu is an online foodstuff marketplace that allows customers to collaborate and share the cost of buying in bulk. By pooling together their resources, Maalu customers can purchase high-quality ingredients at a lower cost than buying them individually.

How does Maalu work?

At Maalu, our customers can easily browse our selection of food items and place orders with just a click of a button. We offer the option for customers to purchase a share of a food item by joining a slot. Additionally, customers can share these slot offers with their friends and family through direct messaging or social media sharing.

Who can use Maalu?

Maalu is open to anyone who is interested in collaborative food buying. Our platform is particularly useful for home cooks, meal prep enthusiasts, and small business owners who want to purchase high-quality ingredients at a lower cost.

What types of foodstuff are available on Maalu?

In addition to meat, Maalu also offers a diverse range of food items such as grains, flour, spices, nuts, and much more. Our selection is regularly updated based on customer demand, so make sure to check back frequently to see what's new!

How is Maalu different from other food marketplaces?

Maalu is unique because of our collaborative buying model, which allows customers to save money and reduce waste by purchasing in bulk. We also prioritize sustainability and transparency, working with farmers and producers who share our values and providing detailed information about the origin and quality of our ingredients.

How can I get started with Maalu?

Creating an account on our app is simple - just sign up and a profile will be created for you. Once you have created an account, you can start browsing our selection of food items. You can find trending slots on the featured page. If the item you require is not available, you can create a slot, and your followers will be notified. You can also share the slot link on various social media channels. Once the slot is created, it will be advertised in the marketplace.

Why must i follow people?

While it's not mandatory to follow other users, following other people aligns with the idea of building a community. By following other users, you will receive notifications when they create or join a group. It makes sense to follow individuals who have purchased the same item as you, as they are more likely to make a purchase again when you are ready to buy next time.

What if I do not want to join a slot?

Although our platform promotes collaborative buying to save money, it is also possible to make a purchase individually. Simply go to the "Create a Slot" option and click on "Solo Purchase," and the item will be sent directly to your personal cart.
